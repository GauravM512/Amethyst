# How to Workspace

The Workspace is the center of your Amethyst Studio project. It is designed around five different modes, each tailored for a specific part of your workflow. You can easily switch between them using the **Workspace Switcher**.

![Workspace Switcher](res://workspace_switcher.png)

*The Workspace Switcher*

## Workspace Modes

### 1. Performance Mode
**Performance Mode** is designed for live use. It provides a clean, streamlined interface for you to trigger interactive elements, launch clips, and perform your project in real-time.

### 2. Timeline Mode
**Timeline Mode** is used for sequencing and arranging. It allows you to create events, draw automation curves, and arrange your lighting clips over time to build a complete show.

### 3. Lights Mode
**Lights Mode** is where you build your chains and design your light effects. Here you can add and route devices to create complex lighting behaviors.

### 4. Sampling Mode
**Sampling Mode** gives you the tools to work with audio files. You can configure and edit your samples here so they perfectly match your light sequences.

### 5. Layout Mode
**Layout Mode** is where you build your physical setup. Here, you can place and arrange your Launchpad devices on a virtual canvas. This mode is essential for mapping out where your devices are in real life.
