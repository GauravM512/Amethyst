# Opacity

The Opacity device sets the opacity of incoming lit signals to a specified level. It's most commonly used to adjust or reduce the overall brightness of an LED pattern or effect.

![Opacity device](res://opacity.jpg)

*Opacity device*

The device features a single control:
* **Opacity Dial**: This dial lets you adjust the desired opacity level, ranging from 0% to 100%. You can turn the dial or click the percentage text to type an exact value.

The device processes the signal by updating the opacity of any incoming lit LED signal to the value selected by the dial. Unlit (off) signals pass through unmodified so they can correctly clear their respective LED slots.
